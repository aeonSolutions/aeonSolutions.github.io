Who Am I?

Hi,
this is the page of Miguel Tomás , an experienced professional in scientific research and smart technology solutions, adeptly combines hardware and software expertise. With a track record of successful remote team management. Since 2018, Miguel moderated Slack, WhatsApp, and LinkedIn groups, where provided valuable advice and mentorship to individuals and organizations. The most recent achievement can be found on his GitHub profile with more than 200 repositories and webpages about open scientific research, prototyping and innovation from a simple concept in a doodle to a fully matured minimum viable product

Noteworthy accomplishments:
■ Pioneering digital transformation at the University of Minho, Laboratory of Construction and Building Materials (2005-09)
■ Driving three digital transformations for start-up enterprises between 2007 and 2020. The most recent a "Digital Transformation on a Small Start-Up Enterprise: Design, Prototype, and Deployment Based on Human-Centered Design Principles for the Construction Sector"
■ Designed, tested, and prototyped over 70 technology solutions
■ Specialized in smart self-sensing carbon-based composites for Structural Health Monitoring (2005-07, 2017 and 2020-23)
  -Designed & prototyped a flexible electrode suitable for data acquisition with carbon fiber composites
■ Successfully designed & prototyped the following automotive solutions:
  -Common injection rail for LPG systems (2014-2016)
  -Open electronics for vehicles with a combustion engine, for instance an open engine management system. (2023-)

Miguel has an extensive portfolio encompassing more than 60 open hardware smart PCB electronics and over 30 open software solutions, available on GitHub repositories: "PCB-Prototyping-Catalogue" and "aeonlabs-open-software-catalogue" respectively.

He possesses a deep commitment to advancing knowledge. He holds a Master's degree in Smart Carbon Fiber Composites for Structural Health Monitoring, a second master degree and 79.5 ECTS in Master-level courses on Artificial Intelligence and Smart IoT devices from Swedish and Spanish universities. To date, he authored five scientific publications and co-authored two.

Miguel is eager to leverage his expertise, drive innovation, and contribute to technological advancements.
